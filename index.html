Here to help you run your restraunt!

HelpBite is a day-to-day restraunt assistant tool that is freely available from a web browser and can be accessed on desktop, tablet, or mobile. By using this applications services you can complete tasks and recieve important information with ease, helping you to have a more productive and successful day of business.

Current Services

Bill Calculator

Allows you to calculate a table bill from your restraunt, this includes tips and a comment from your customer.

Food Alerts

Gives you the latest alerts from the food standards agency, ensuring your customers are safe.

Order & Info

Makes it easy to order more ingredients for your restraunt, select from your most frequent supliers.
